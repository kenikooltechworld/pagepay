# PagePay: Third-Party Revenue Opportunities (Nigeria-Focused)

**Date**: July 5, 2026  
**Purpose**: Expand revenue streams by integrating third-party services where users earn money, platform takes a cut, and third parties pay the commission.

---

## Business Model

**Flow:**
1. User completes action through PagePay (survey, purchase, signup, referral)
2. Third-party service pays PagePay commission
3. PagePay splits commission with user (typically 60-70% to user, 30-40% to platform)
4. User earns points, platform profits, third party gets customer/data

**Key Principle**: Platform NEVER pays from own pocket. All rewards funded by third-party commissions.

## 🚨 LEGAL & COMPLIANCE

### Required Registrations:
1. **Affiliate Programs**: Sign up for Jumia Partner, Konga Affiliate, etc.
2. **Fintech Partnerships**: Formal agreements with Opay, Kuda (KYC verification)
3. **Crypto**: Register as affiliate partner (no license needed for referrals)
4. **Betting**: Gambling advertising license (if promoting betting)

### Data Privacy:
- User referrals must opt-in
- Disclose commission structure in ToS
- NDPR compliance (Nigerian Data Protection Regulation)

### Tax:
- Declare commission income (corporate tax)
- Withhold tax on user payouts if > ₦100,000/month per user
